Full body exfoliation massage

A full body exfoliation massage, also known as a body scrub, removes old and dull skin cells to reveal soft and healthy skin underneath.

This treatment accelerates skin turnover and stimulates collagen production for a youthful and elastic skin texture.

Here’s what to expect:
• Step 1: Strip down (full privacy assured, underwear can be worn).
• Step 2: Get exfoliated by your aesthetician.
• Step 3: Get rinsed to remove exfoliants.
• Step 4: Get moisturized with lotion or oil for silky smooth skin.
